My philosophy: Master the PM craft, adapt tech fluently, solve real problems. I'm naturally adaptable to emerging technologies and actively integrating AI/ML concepts, workflow automation (n8n), and Gen AI tools (GPT-4) to enhance governance, reporting, and team effectiveness.
